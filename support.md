# LiftBlock Support

LiftBlock is a strength-training app developed by Adam Zach.

If you have a question, run into an issue, or need help with anything, get in touch and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

**Email:** [support.liftblock@gmail.com](mailto:support.liftblock@gmail.com)

## Common questions

**How do I back up my programs?**
Sign in or create a free account from the Settings tab. Once signed in, your programs are automatically backed up and will sync across your devices.

**How do I cancel my Premium subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. On your iPhone, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, find LiftBlock, and tap Cancel Subscription.

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to Settings → Delete Account inside the app, or email us at [support.liftblock@gmail.com](mailto:support.liftblock@gmail.com) and we will delete your account and all associated data.

**I lost my programs — what do I do?**
If you were signed in, your programs are safely in the cloud. Sign back in and they will restore automatically. If you were not signed in, programs are stored only on your device and cannot be recovered after uninstalling. We recommend signing in to keep your data safe.

**Something else?**
Email us at [support.liftblock@gmail.com](mailto:support.liftblock@gmail.com) and we will help.
