How do I back up my programs?

Sign in or create a free account from the Settings tab. Once signed in, your programs are automatically backed up to the cloud and will sync across your devices.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play. On iPhone, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions, find LiftBlock, and tap Cancel Subscription. On Android, open the Play Store, tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account inside the app. This permanently deletes your account and all cloud data. Alternatively, email support.liftblock@gmail.com and we will delete it for you.

I lost my programs — what do I do?

If you were signed in, your programs are safely stored in the cloud. Sign back in and they will restore automatically. If you were not signed in, programs are stored only on your device and cannot be recovered after uninstalling. We recommend signing in to keep your data safe.

The app won't load / something isn't working

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection — some features require a connection on first use. If you're still having trouble, email support.liftblock@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device type.
